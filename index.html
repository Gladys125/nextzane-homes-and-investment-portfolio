Our Services

Residential Property Sales

Nextzane Homes & Investment offers a wide range of residential properties designed to meet different lifestyle needs and budgets. We help clients find and purchase the perfect property, including:

(1) Residential lands
(2) Plots in planned estates
(3) Houses and apartments
(4) Luxury homes and duplexes.

Every property sold through Nextzane Homes & Investment is carefully verified to ensure proper ownership and secure documentation.
We assist clients in obtaining and processing essential property documents such as:

(i) Certificate of Occupancy (C of O)

(iv) Survey Plan
(v) Land Title Registration

Our team ensures that every property transaction is transparent, legally compliant, and secure.
